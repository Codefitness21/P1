Hello, my name is...

I create solutions through code.

MySpace??? Whatchu talkin' bout'!? ...Well, my appetite for web development awakened during the MySpace era. After evacuating from Hurricane Katrina in New Orleans, I found myself in Killeen, TX, with ample time on my hands to contemplate my next steps. During this period of uncertainty, I immersed myself in connecting with family and friends through MySpace. In what can only be described as the company's "Happy Accident," fate granted several users access to the CSS code.This unexpected opportunity sparked an excitement within me, marking the budding stages of my journey towards becoming a web developer...Ohhhhh okay.

After setting aside that notion, I dove into a 15-year stint in personal training, even earning a Masters in Nutrition along the way. But my coding interest was reignited during an educational tech course at Texas State University, where I built my first blog in WordPress. Realizing this was my calling, I embarked on learning frontend development solo, dodging more student loan debt...ya feel me? After four years of self-training, I clinched a role at Digital Seat Media â€” my ultimate happy place. Outside of my digital world, you can catch me in my 'sweat box' garage lifting weights, meditating & journaling, which helps keep me at the speed of Zen. When not immersed in books or advancing in Taekwondo, I'm bonding with loved ones, taking walks and playing with my dog, Ankles. My ultimate goal is to evolve into a well-rounded full-stack web developer but for now, feast your eyes on this portfolio I crafted from the dust of creativity using these wonderful ingredients! If only I had captured a timelapse of its birth!
