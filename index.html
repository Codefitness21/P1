Hello, my name is...

I am a Junior Front End Developer and UI Designer.

My interest in health and nutrition evolved throughout the years from my days as a student-athlete to working as a personal trainer. Achieving optimum health has always been a focal point in my life which emanates primarily from my parents who I view as very health conscious. My mother, who is a registered nurse and the pioneer of health and nutrition in our family, has always emphasized the importance of eating a balanced meal and engaging in exercise.

Throughout my career as a fitness professional, I have learned and used a wide range of technological tools, one being a blog I created as a nutrition graduate student for my educational technology course using WordPress. This tool among others inspired me to dive deeper into technology. Initially, I was interested in combining health and fitness with technology to facilitate client improvement in exercise and nutrition. However, since retiring from personal training, my focus has been on developing my skill set in frontend development.

Through workshops, classes, networking, and hands-on learning, I have developed a working skill set for front end web development and designing as it pertains to , , , , , and while continuing to expand my knowledge in . I also continuously seek more related experiences and knowledge in other areas of software development as it relates to back end development with the intention of becoming a full-stack web developer.
