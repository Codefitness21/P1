Hello, my name is...

I am a UI Designer and Web Developer.

My appetite for web development awakened during the MySpace era. After evacuating from Hurricane Katrina in New Orleans, I found myself in Killeen, TX, with ample time on my hands to contemplate my next steps. During this period of uncertainty, I immersed myself in connecting with family and friends through MySpace. In what can only be described as the company's "Happy Accident," fate granted several users, myself included, access to HTML and CSS code. This unexpected opportunity sparked an exhilarating excitement within me, marking the budding stages of my journey towards becoming a web developer.

I didn't dive headfirst into the tech realm right away. For several years, I pursued a career as a personal trainer, armed with a Masters degree in Nutrition. Throughout my career as a fitness professional, I learned and used a wide range of technological tools, one being a blog I created as a nutrition graduate student for my educational technology course using WordPress. This tool among others inspired me to dive deeper into technology. Initially, I was interested in combining health and fitness with technology to facilitate client improvement in exercise and nutrition. However, since retiring from personal training, my focus has been on developing my skill set in frontend development.

Through workshops, classes, networking, and hands-on learning, I have developed a working skill set for frontend web development and designing as it pertains to , , , , , and while continuing to expand my knowledge in . As my expertise expands, I consistently pursue additional experiences and knowledge in various facets of software development, particularly in the realm of backend development. My ultimate goal is to evolve into a well-rounded full-stack web developer.
